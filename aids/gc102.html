Item Listing
by

Graphic Arts Collection
Department of Rare Books and Special Collections

Consists of prints, drawings, and sculptures by Rodin. Included are drawings of nudes, drypoint sketches for La Ronde, lithographs, and two bronze sculptures of Honoré de Balzac. Also included is a woodcut portrait of Rodin, drawn by Rene Cheruy and engraved by Germaine Rouget Cheruy.
