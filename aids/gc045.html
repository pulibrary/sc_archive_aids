James Abbott McNeill Whistler Collection

(GC045)

Item Listing
by
Vicki Principi
 


Graphic Arts Collection
Department of Rare Books and Special Collections
Princeton University Library
2005


Consists of prints created by Whistler. Included in the collection are etchings from Whistler's French Set, Thames Set, First Venice Set, and Second Venice Set; lithographs printed by T. R. Way and published in The Studio; and wood engravings published in Once a Week.
