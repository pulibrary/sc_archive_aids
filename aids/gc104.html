Consists of two portfolios of prints created by local artists as part of Princeton's celebration of the American Revolution Bicentennial. One portfolio, titled "The Ten Crucial Days" (1976) and edited by Judith Brodsky and Helen Schwartz, is number 15 of an edition of 20; the other, titled "1776-1976, A Portfolio" (1978) and edited by Zelda Laschever, is number 5 of an edition of 20.
