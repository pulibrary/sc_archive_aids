Austrian Prints Collection

(GC121)
 

Item Listing
by
Vicki Principi
 

Graphic Arts Collection
Department of Rare Books and Special Collections
Princeton University Library
2004


Consists of prints by various Austrian artists, dating mostly from the first half of the 20th century and demonstrating a wide variety of printing techniques: woodcut, drypoint, etching, aquatint, and engraving.
