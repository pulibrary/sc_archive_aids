Consists of 59 pen-and-ink drawings by Noell primarily for book ornamentation. Included are his designs for Joseph and His Brothers, The Essays of Michel De Montaigne (New York: Alfred A. Knopf, 1934), and The Romance of Leonardo Da Vinci (New York: Random House, 1931).
