Dutch Prints Collection

(GC070)
 

Item Listing
by
Vicki Principi
 

Graphic Arts Collection
Department of Rare Books and Special Collections
Princeton University Library
2004


Consists of prints by various Dutch artists, dating from the early 16th century through the mid-20th century. The collection includes numerous works by Hendrick Goltzius, Jacobus Houbraken, Adriaan Lubbers, Rembrandt van Rijn, Reinier Vinkeles, and Cornelis de Visscher. Printing techniques represented in the collection include drypoint, engraving, etching, linocut, lithograph, and mezzotint.
