Philippine Costume Drawings Collection

(GC120)

Item Listing
by
Vicki Principi

Graphic Arts Collection
Department of Rare Books and Special Collections
Princeton University Library
2005


Consists of 16 matted watercolor drawings of costumes worn by men and women of the Philippines in the middle of the nineteenth century. Four of the drawings are signed by the artist "Justiniano."

Box 1 contents [Entire collection]:

Drawings signed by artist Justiniano and dated 1843
Untitled [Man in red, white, and blue attire; wearing brimmed tan hat and carrying stick]
Untitled [Woman in blue and green plaid skirt, orange and white striped shirt, white shawl; holding closed fan]

Untitled [Woman in blue apron, plaid skirt, pink and white striped shirt, white shawl; holding scarf]

Unsigned drawings with no dates
