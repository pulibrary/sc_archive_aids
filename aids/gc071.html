Eugene Berman Collection

(GC071)

Item Listing
by
Vicki Principi

Graphic Arts Collection
Department of Rare Books and Special Collections
Princeton University Library
2005


Consists of pen-and-ink and wash designs by Berman for theatrical sets and costumes. Included are: three set designs for productions of Romeo & Juliet and Igor Stravinsky's Giselle and Danses concertantes; three costume designs; and exhibition ephemera.

Box 1 contents:
Giselle, Act I [set design]; pen and ink, grisaille; n.d.

Box 2 contents:
Danses concertantes, Igor Stravinsky [set design]; pen and ink, grisaille; n.d.

Box 3 contents:
Danses concertantes [Male costume, tree-like design]; pen and ink with wash; 1944
Eugene Berman exhibition at Princeton University Library: Poster; 1947
Eugene Berman exhibition at Princeton University Library: Program; 1947
Eugene Berman exhibition at Princeton University Library: Program cover design; wash; 1947
Romeo and Juliet. Central construction of the main setting.; pen and ink, grisaille and wash; 1942
Romeo finale [Male costume design]; pen and ink, grisaille and wash; 1943
Untitled [Costume design, winged figure]; pen and ink; 1947
