Graphic Arts Collection
Department of Rare Books and Special Collections
Princeton University Library

Consists of prints created by German artists. Print media represented in the collection include: woodcuts; linocuts; wood, copper, and steel engravings; etchings; aquatints; mezzotints; drypoints; photogravures; and lithographs.
