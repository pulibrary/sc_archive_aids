Consists of drawings, prints, printing plates, and fabrics created by Rockwell Kent, as well as ephemera related to his work. The collection includes illustrations published in All Men Are Enemies, Architec-tonics, Basket of Poses, Beowulf, Bridge of San Luis Rey, Candide, Candy, Canterbury Tales, Dreams and Derisions, Erewhon, Harper's Weekly, Moby Dick, N by E, New York Sunday Tribune, Rollo in Society, Vanity Fair, and Wilderness. In addition, there are bookplates, book mock-ups, tableware, and the manuscript for an unpublished work entitled "A Rosary."

For additional Rockwell Kent holdings within the Princeton University Library, see Elmer Adler Collection CO262.

Copyrights to the artwork and writings of Rockwell Kent are owned by the Plattsburgh College Foundation for the benefit of the Plattsburgh State Art Museum, Rockwell Kent Collection. Requests for permission to use Kent work in the Princeton Collection should be sent to Cecilia Esposito, Plattsburgh State Art Museum, 101 Broad Street, Plattsburgh, NY 12901.
