Flemish Prints Collection

(GC090)
 

Item listing
by
Vicki Principi
 

Graphic Arts Collection
Department of Rare Books and Special Collections
Princeton University Library
2004


Consists of prints by various Flemish artists, dating from the mid-16th century through the late 17th century. The collection includes numerous works by Gerard Edelinck, Paulus Pontius, Cornelis Schut, Lucas Emil Vorsterman, and Jeronimus Wierix. Printing techniques represented in the collection include etching and engraving.
