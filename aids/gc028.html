Fritz Eichenberg Collection

Item Listing
by

Graphic Arts Collection
Department of Rare Books and Special Collections
Princeton University Library
2004


Consists of wood engravings and lithographs by Eichenberg, spanning his career. Includes illustrations for Das Volksbuch von till Eulenspiegel (Leipzig: 1922), Gulliver's Travels (Defoe, Leipzig: 1922), The Brothers Karamazov (Dostoevsky, New York: Limited Editions Club, 1949), A Raw Youth (Dostoevsky, New York: Limited Editions Club, 1974), The House of the Dead (Dostoevsky, New York: Limited Editions Club, 1982), Rainbows Are Made (Carl Sandburg, New York: Harcourt Brace Jovanovich, 1982), The Diary of a Country Priest (George Bernanos, New York: Limited Editions Club, 1986), and unpublished engravings for The Once and Future King (T. H. White).
