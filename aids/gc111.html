Bookbinding Collection

(GC111)
 

Item Listing
by
Vicki Principi
 

Graphic Arts Collection
Department of Rare Books and Special Collections
Princeton University Library
2004

Contains bookbinding specimens, vendor samples, and prints of decorative bookbindings. The specimens include a filigree binding, early and modern oak boards, paper boards, clamshell boxes, and decorative paper and leather bindings. Dates and artists are unknown.
