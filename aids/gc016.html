Robert Nanteuil Collection

(GC016)

Item Listing
by
Vicki Principi

Graphic Arts Collection
Department of Rare Books and Special Collections
Princeton University Library
2005


A collection of portrait engravings by Robert Nanteuil including three portraits of Louis XIV, two of Anne of Austria, a portrait on silk of Philippe, Duke d'Orleans, as well as portraits of Richelieu, Mazarin, Colbert, Fouquet, and other members of the French Court. [Digital images]
