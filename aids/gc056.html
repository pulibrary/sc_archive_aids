Jonathan Bumas Collection

(GC056)

Listing
by
Vicki Principi

Graphic Arts Collection
Department of Rare Books and Special Collections
Princeton University Library
2004

Consists of seven book jackets for contemporary fiction and nonfiction, a letter to John Bidwell, a 1999 New Year's greeting card, a pencil portrait of Bumas' nine-week-old son (Adam), and an eight-page humorous booklet written about Adam. All items were created by Bumas.

Contents:

Dancing on the bridge of Avignon, by Ida Vos [dust jacket]
F. Scott Fitzgerald: The Princeton years, selected writings 1914-1920, ed. by Chip Deffaa [dust jacket]
Nell's quilt, by Susan Terris [dust jacket]
Other minds, critical essays 1969-1994, by Thomas Nagel [dust jacket]
Paradise for the Portuguese queen, poems by Benjamin Ivry [dust jacket]
The pentum mission, by Joe Fontana [dust jacket]
The price of tea in China, by E. Shaskan Bumas [dust jacket]
New Year's greeting card; 1999
Adam Hollander Bumas sleeping at 9 1/2 weeks; 1998 [portrait]
We test the new '99 Adam 1.0; 1999 [booklet]
Letter to John Bidwell [curator of Princeton University Library's Graphic Arts Dept.]; 1999
