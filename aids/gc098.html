Consists of prints created by 20th-century Spanish artists. The collection includes works by Ricardo Baroja y Nessi, Maureen Lucia Booth, Antonio Clave, Salvador Dali, Cruz Diez, Joan Miro, and Pablo Picasso. Printing techniques represented in the collection include aquatint, etching, lithograph, and screen print.
