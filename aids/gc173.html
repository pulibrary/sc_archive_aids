Italian Drawings and Paintings Collection

(GC173)

Item Listing
by
Vicki Principi

Graphic Arts Collection
Department of Rare Books and Special Collections
Princeton University Library
2005


Consists of gouache paintings of the sea and landscape around Naples, Italy; watercolors of the Middle East by Luigi Mayer; two pencil portraits by Carlo Pellegrini; a pen-and-ink drawing of a boy by Sabatini; a pen-and-wash drawing of a Catholic church interior by Paoletti; and a pencil drawing of the Mosque of Hebron by Sierotti.
