Process Printing Collection

(GC134)
 

Listing
by
Vicki Principi
 

Graphic Arts Collection
Department of Rate Book and Special Collections
Princeton University Library
2004

Contains instructional leaflets with examples of the following photographic printing techniques: aquatone, four-color, half-tone, line cut, photogelatin, photogravure, and rotogravure. The collection also includes a collotype; photogravure portraits; photographic prints of panoramic views; and modern reproductions after Lionel Barrymore, Albrecht Durer, and James McNeill Whistler.
