Consists of drawings by Von Schmidt: four pen-and-ink ornamental page designs, one frontispiece, and eight pen-and-ink illustrations for Willa Cather's "'December Night': A Scene from Death Comes for the Archbishop" (New York: A. A. Knopf, 1933), printed by the Pynson Printers under the supervision of Elmer Adler.

Box 1 contents [Entire collection]:

Page 6, Archbishop with arm around cloaked figure; pen and ink, tempera
Page 9, Robed figure near mule; pen and ink, tempera
Page 10, Crowd watching figure pass by; pen and ink, tempera
Page 11, Children watching musicians; pen and ink, tempera
Virgin and child; pen and ink, tempera
Untitled [Ornamental border with one cherub]; brown ink, tempera
